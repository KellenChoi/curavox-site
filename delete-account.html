Hana - Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Follow the steps below to delete your Hana account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Hana app on your phone
  2. Go to the Settings tab (gear icon)
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Sign Out
  4. To permanently delete your account and all data, email us at: privacy@cura-vox.com
  5. Include the email address you used to create your account
  6. We will delete your account and all data within 7 business days

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your profile (name, language preference, settings)
  • All conversation history with Hana
  • All memories Hana learned about you
  • All contacts saved in the app
  • All reminders (medication, appointments)
  • Health profile (medications, conditions, symptoms)
  • Mood history and feedback data
  • Family member connections and alerts
Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data listed above will be permanently removed from our servers.

Questions?
