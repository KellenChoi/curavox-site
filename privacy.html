Hana - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

하나 - 개인정보 처리방침 (시행일: 2026년 4월 9일)

Curavox ("we", "us", "our") operates the Hana mobile application ("Hana", "the App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use Hana.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Email address and display name provided during registration.

Voice Data: When you speak to Hana, your voice is temporarily processed for speech-to-text conversion. We do not store raw audio recordings. Only the text transcript is saved.

Conversation History: Text transcripts of your conversations with Hana are stored to provide continuity and context across sessions.

Health Information: Medications, conditions, allergies, and symptoms you voluntarily share with Hana. This information is stored to build your health profile.

Memories: Facts you share (family names, preferences, schedules) that help Hana assist you better.

Mood Data: Emotional state indicators derived from conversations to help Hana provide appropriate support.

Location Data: Approximate location (GPS) used only for weather information and emergency service routing (911/119). Location is not tracked continuously.

Device Contacts: Accessed only when you ask Hana to call or text someone not in your Hana contacts. Contact data is not uploaded or stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve Hana's AI assistant services
  • To remember your preferences, health information, and personal context
  • To set and deliver medication and appointment reminders
  • To generate health trend insights and wellness check-ins
  • To share alerts with family members you explicitly authorize
  • To route emergency calls to the appropriate local number

3. Data Sharing

Family Members: If you choose to add family members, they can view your reminders, health profile, and receive wellness alerts. You control who has access.

Third-Party Services: We use the following services to operate Hana:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication (data encrypted at rest)
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI conversation processing (conversations are not used to train AI models)
  • Deepgram — Speech-to-text conversion (audio is not retained)
  • ElevenLabs — Text-to-speech conversion (text is not retained)

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored on Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Access to your data is restricted by row-level security policies — only you (and authorized family members) can access your information.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Hana is designed for adult users, particularly elderly individuals. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for family sharing at any time

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

Curavox
Email: privacy@curavox.com
